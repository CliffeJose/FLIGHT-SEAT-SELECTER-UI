Project: Flight Booking Platform

Overview:
This platform allows users to book flight seats from Bangkok, Thailand, to various destinations. Users can select their destination, choose a flight date, and pick seats from an interactive plane map.

Select Destination:

Choose your destination from a list.
The ticket price (in USD) per seat will be displayed in the bottom summary.
Select Flight Date:

Pick the desired flight date from a calendar interface.
Seat Selection:

View the seat map of the plane with different seat statuses:
Unavailable seats are shown in red and cannot be selected.
Available seats are shown in gray.
When a seat is selected, it turns green.
The number of selected seats and the total price will automatically update in the bottom summary.
